Installing the Mod

Ninja AmongUs: Active Roles

Crewmates

Can use the vents to travel around the map.

Can watch a player to see who visits them.

Can store votes and release them all at once in a meeting.

Can grant a player a one-time shield that prevents a kill.

Can hear from dead players during meetings.

Can check if a dead player's alignment was Good or Evil.

Can investigate a player to see their exact role.

Learns Impostors' identities after finishing their tasks.

Can place a trap that reveals who triggered it.

Impostors

Can prevent a player from speaking during meetings.

Can plant a remote bomb on another player to kill them.

Can cause a total darkness where only Impostors can see.

Can guess a player's role, and if correct, remove them from the game.

Can clean a body, making it unreportable.

Can place temporary vents anywhere on the map.

Can drag a body, which will auto-report after some time.

Neutrals

Can remember the role of a dead player and take on their goal.

Wins if their specific target is voted out.

Wins if their linked target survives to the end of the game.

Wins if they get voted out by the other players.
