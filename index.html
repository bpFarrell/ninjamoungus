Installing the Mod

  • • Download this file (TouMira-v1.5.9-x86-steam-itch.zip)
  • • In Steam right click on Among Us and goto Manage / Browse Local Files.
  • • Extract the contents of the zip folder into the Among Us Directory
  • • Run the Among Us through Steam (First time loading will take a min.)
